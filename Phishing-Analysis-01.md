Phishing Email Analysis Lab 
Scenario

In this lab, I analyzed a suspicious email reported by a user to determine if it was a phishing attempt and identify potential Indicators of Compromise (IoCs).
Tools Used

    Wireshark (Network traffic analysis)

    Any.Run / VirusTotal (Link & File analysis)

    CyberChef (Decoding headers)

Analysis Steps

    Header Analysis: Checked the 'From' address and 'Return-Path' to detect spoofing.

    Link Inspection: Extracted URLs and checked their reputation on VirusTotal.

    Attachment Analysis: Analyzed the file hash of the attached document.
